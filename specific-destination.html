overview

For the adventurous family, Vietnam offers dramatic scenery, ancient temples and palaces, fantastic and easy cycling and great beaches. If you are looking to just fly and flop then head instead to Thailand, but if you want to explore, then travelling from North to South, Vietnam offers buzzing Hanoi, the majestic Halong Bay, picturesque countryside, the Imperial City of Hue, the sleepy riverside Hoi An, the Mekong Delta and lots of beaches on the way.
